About Me

Born and raised in the Los Angeles area, Chelsea moved to Brooklyn to pursue filmmaking at The Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, NY. In 2013, Chelsea graduated with a BFA in Critical Studies, focusing on political theory, filmmaking, and psychology. After graduating Chelsea proceeded to work in the commercial film industry for 4 years before deciding to make a change. Now Chelsea is learning how to code through The Software Guild.

As an envoronmentalist and a vegan who loves to travel, Chelsea hopes to find engineering work for a company that shares her values.
